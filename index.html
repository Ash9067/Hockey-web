Hockey Training

The frequency of off-ice training depends on your level of play and goals. Generally, 3-5 sessions per week, including a mix of strength, cardio, and skill-specific exercises, is recommended.

Private Lessons

Private lessons can cover a wide range of skills, including skating technique, puck handling, shooting, passing, positioning, and game strategy.

Ski Touring

Hockey ski touring combines elements of cross-country skiing and hockey. Players use ski equipment to navigate snow-covered terrain while carrying hockey sticks and a puck, blending the excitement of hockey with the challenges of skiing

Booking

Contact your local ice rink or facility directly, either by phone or through their online booking system. Many facilities offer online platforms for convenient scheduling.

Pricing

Recreational hockey costs can vary widely depending on factors such as location, facility type, and league fees. Local adult leagues may range from a few hundred to over a thousand dollars per season.
